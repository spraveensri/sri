Computer Lab

Computer science is taught as a compulsory subject that spans over the elementary, middle, secondary and senior secondary level, our computer lab is equipped with 30 computers and is open to all students from Grade 1 to Grade 12. All computers have access to application for word processing, spreadsheet, presentation, database, Programming C and C++. Our computer department offers 24 hrs internets and ups facilities. Student may work in the lab on subject related projects, under the supervision of respective subject teacher
